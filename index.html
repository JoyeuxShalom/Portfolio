My Services

Penetration Testing

I offer penetration testing services to identify and exploit vulnerabilities in your systems. By simulating real-world attacks, I help you strengthen your security measures and protect your digital assets from potential threats. My approach involves thorough reconnaissance, vulnerability scanning, and exploiting weaknesses to assess the effectiveness of your security measures.

Incident Response & Analysis

I offer services to help organizations swiftly and effectively manage security incidents. By identifying, containing, and mitigating threats, I ensure minimal impact on operations. My analysis focuses on understanding the root cause, attack vectors, and potential impacts, providing actionable insights to enhance your security posture and prevent future incidents.

Tools Configuration

I offer configuration of security tools like SIEM systems, IDS/IPS, and firewalls to enhance your organization's defenses. My services ensure optimal setup and performance, aligning with industry standards to detect threats and respond effectively. With tailored configurations, your network remains secure against potential breaches.
